DeathCheck:
WAIT MIL 100
GOTO GenDeath $IF {GENERATORS:ENGAGED} = 3
GOTO WarheadDeath $IF {WARHEAD:ISDETONATED} = TRUE
GOTO DeathCheck

GenDeath:
ELEVATOR UNLOCK *
ELEVATOR SEND *
CASSIE LOUD SCP 2 3 3 8 .G3 jam_013_2 CONTAINEDSUCCESSFULLY BY Generator .G6 Override .|SCP-2338 contained successfully by Generator Override.
STOP SCP-2338
STOP

WarheadDeath:
ELEVATOR UNLOCK *
ELEVATOR SEND *
CASSIE LOUD SCP 2 3 3 8 CONTAINEDSUCCESSFULLY BY .G5 ALPHA .G2 WARHEAD .|SCP-2338 contained successfully by Alpha Warhead.
STOP SCP-2338
STOP
